What Is a Plagiarism Checker?

A plagiarism checker analyzes your text for similarity to existing content. Students use it to verify their work is original before submission. Writers use it to ensure their content is unique. Teachers use it to check student submissions for copied material.

How to Check for Plagiarism

  • Paste your text into the box above
  • Click "Check for Plagiarism"
  • Review your originality score and highlighted phrases
  • Rewrite any flagged sections in your own words

Tips for Writing Original Content

  • Paraphrase in your own words — don't copy and paste, even with citation
  • Cite your sources — always credit where ideas come from
  • Use quotes for direct language — exact quotes must be in quotation marks
  • Check before submitting — run a plagiarism check on every assignment
